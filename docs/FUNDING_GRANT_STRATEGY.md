## 2. Funding Strategy Overview

### 2.1 Multi-Track Approach

We're pursuing a **diversified funding strategy** to minimize risk and maximize resources:

**Track 1: Grants (Non-dilutive)**
- Government innovation grants
- Accessibility-focused grants
- Small business development funds
- Social impact grants

**Track 2: Angel Investment**
- Individual angel investors
- Angel syndicates
- Design/accessibility industry angels

**Track 3: Venture Capital**
- Pre-seed/seed stage VCs
- SaaS-focused funds
- Impact investors

**Track 4: Strategic Investment**
- Corporate venture arms (Figma, Adobe)
- Accessibility companies
- Design tool companies

**Track 5: Alternative Funding**
- Revenue-based financing
- Accelerator programs
- Crowdfunding (Kickstarter/IndieGoGo)

### 2.2 Funding Stages

**Bootstrap Phase (Current - Month 3):**
- **Funding:** Founder capital, customer revenue
- **Goal:** Reach $10,000 MRR, prove product-market fit
- **Burn:** $2,000/month (minimal, founder time only)

**Pre-Seed Phase (Months 4-9):**
- **Funding Target:** $50,000 - $150,000
- **Source:** Grants + angel investors
- **Goal:** Reach $50,000 MRR, hire 1-2 team members
- **Burn:** $15,000/month

**Seed Phase (Months 10-18):**
- **Funding Target:** $500,000 - $1,500,000
- **Source:** VC + strategic investors
- **Goal:** Reach $200,000 MRR, team of 5-8
- **Burn:** $75,000/month

**Series A Phase (Months 19-36):**
- **Funding Target:** $3,000,000 - $8,000,000
- **Source:** Top-tier VCs
- **Goal:** $2M ARR, market leadership
- **Burn:** $250,000/month

### 6.2 Unit Economics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
- Organic channels: ~$2 per user
- Paid channels: ~$15 per user
- Blended CAC: ~$10 per user

**Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Average subscription lifetime: 18 months
- Average revenue per user: $9/month
- LTV: 18 √ó $9 = $162

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** 16:1 (Excellent - target >3:1)

**Gross Margin:**
- Revenue: $9/month per user
- COGS: $1.35/month (hosting, payment processing, support)
- Gross Margin: 85%

**Payback Period:**
- CAC / (Monthly ARPU √ó Gross Margin)
- $10 / ($9 √ó 0.85) = 1.3 months (Target <12 months ‚úì)
